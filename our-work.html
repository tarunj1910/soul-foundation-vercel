Our Activities

Soul Foundation carries out activities focused on supporting children studying at the Mentally Retarded School and caring for elderly people in need. Through education, daily care, food support and community service, we strive to improve lives and provide comfort, dignity and hope to those who depend on our support.

Mentally Retarded School

Soul Foundation supports children studying at the Mentally Retarded School through care, education and daily assistance. Every morning, children are picked up from their homes in specially arranged school vans and safely brought to the school.

Every morning, children are picked up from their homes in specially arranged school vans and safely brought to the school. At the school they receive special education, learning activities and individual attention according to their needs.Throughout the day, trained teachers and staff provide supervision and support. Assistance is given for daily activities such as eating, personal care and using the restroom whenever required. Nutritious food and refreshments are also provided.Students take part in classroom learning, drawing, games, music, cultural programmes and other activities that help improve confidence, communication and social interaction.At the end of the day, the children are safely dropped back home in the school vans. Our aim is to provide a safe, caring and supportive environment where every child receives the attention and care they deserve.

Elderly Care & Assistance

Soul Foundation also works for elderly people who need care, support and companionship. Many elderly people face loneliness, health challenges and financial difficulties. Through our activities, we provide assistance, care and emotional support to help them live with dignity and comfort.

We also organise food distribution programmes for elderly people, poor families and individuals in need. By providing meals and essential support, we aim to bring relief and hope to people facing difficult circumstances. Our mission is simple – to ensure that no elderly person feels forgotten and no one goes hungry when help can be provided.

By promoting awareness, engagement, and assistance, we strive to enhance the quality of life for elderly individuals and ensure that they continue to receive the care and recognition they deserve.

Food Distribution Programs

Access to nutritious food is a fundamental human need. Through food distribution initiatives and community outreach programs, Soul Foundation seeks to support individuals and families facing hardship and food insecurity.

Our efforts are focused on ensuring that vulnerable individuals receive assistance during difficult circumstances and are treated with compassion, respect, and dignity.

We believe that even a simple meal can provide comfort, hope, and encouragement during challenging times.

Community Welfare Activities

Beyond individual support programs, Soul Foundation actively engages in broader community welfare activities aimed at improving social well-being and fostering a culture of compassion and responsibility.

Through awareness campaigns, outreach activities, volunteer engagement, and community-based initiatives, we work to strengthen social connections and create positive opportunities for people in need.

Our goal is to contribute to stronger, healthier, and more inclusive communities where everyone has the opportunity to succeed and flourish.

Future Activities and Expansion Plan

As Soul Foundation grows, we aspire to expand our impact through healthcare assistance programs, educational support initiatives, skill development opportunities, rehabilitation services, women empowerment activities, and additional community welfare programs. Our long-term vision is to build sustainable initiatives that create meaningful and lasting positive change across society.
