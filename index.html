About Soul Foundation

Our Mission

Soul Foundation is a charitable trust dedicated to uplifting the lives of vulnerable and neglected individuals in society.

We support mentally challenged children who are often ignored, abandoned or misunderstood and provide them emotional care, rehabilitation support and a loving environment.

We also care for elderly people who are lonely, neglected or left without support, helping them live with dignity and compassion.

In addition, we provide food for needy street dwellers and people suffering from hunger and poverty because nobody deserves to sleep hungry.

As our trust grows, we aspire to expand our services into healthcare assistance, rehabilitation programs, education support, women empowerment, skill development and broader community welfare initiatives.

What We Do

Our Core Initiatives

We are committed to serving society through compassion, support, nourishment and humanity.

Support for Mentally Challenged Children

We provide emotional care, rehabilitation, guidance and support for mentally challenged children who are often ignored or abandoned.

Elderly Care

We care for elderly people who are neglected, lonely or abandoned and ensure they receive love, food, care and emotional support.

We distribute food to needy street dwellers and hungry individuals because no human being deserves to sleep hungry.
