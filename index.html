Welcome to Soul Foundation

Soul Foundation was started in 2023 to support children studying at the Mentally Retarded School, care for elderly people and help those in need. We believe that every person deserves love, respect, care and an opportunity to live with dignity. Through our daily activities and community service programmes, we work to make a positive difference in the lives of mentally retarded children, elderly people and families who need support. Our aim is not only to provide help but also to create a caring environment where people feel valued, supported and included.

Our Activities

Mentally Retarded School

Soul Foundation supports children studying at the Mentally Retarded School through care, education and daily assistance. Every morning, the children are picked up from their homes in specially arranged school vans and safely brought to the school. At the school, they receive special education, training activities and individual attention according to their needs. The children are cared for throughout the day by trained teachers and staff members.

Assistance is provided for daily activities such as eating, personal care and using the restroom whenever required. Nutritious food and refreshments are also provided to support their health and well-being. Apart from classroom learning, the children participate in drawing, games, music, cultural programmes and other activities that help improve confidence, communication and social skills. At the end of the day, the children are safely dropped back to their homes in the school vans. Our goal is to provide a caring, safe and supportive environment where every child feels valued and receives the attention they deserve.

Elderly Care & Food Support

Soul Foundation also works for elderly people who need care, support and companionship.

Many elderly people face loneliness, health challenges and financial difficulties. Through our activities, we provide assistance, care and emotional support to help them live with dignity and comfort.

We also organise food distribution programmes for elderly people, poor families and individuals in need. By providing meals and essential support, we aim to bring relief and hope to people facing difficult circumstances.

Our mission is simple – to ensure that no elderly person feels forgotten and no one goes hungry when help can be provided.
