About Soul Foundation

Soul Foundation is a charitable trust established in 2023 with the aim of supporting children studying at the Mentally Retarded School, caring for elderly people and helping those in need. Through education, daily care, food support and community service, we work to make a positive difference in people's lives. Our goal is to ensure that every person we serve is treated with care, respect and dignity.

Our Story

Soul Foundation was established in 2023 with the aim of supporting children studying at the Mentally Retarded School, caring for elderly people and helping those in need. What started as a simple desire to serve the community has grown into a dedicated effort to provide education, daily care, food support and assistance to vulnerable individuals. Through compassion, commitment and service, the Foundation strives to bring comfort, hope and dignity to the lives of those it serves. We believe that every child deserves care and encouragement, every elderly person deserves respect and support, and every individual facing difficulties deserves the opportunity to live with dignity and hope.

From supporting individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities to extending care for elderly citizens and providing food assistance to people experiencing hardship, Soul Foundation seeks to make a meaningful difference through consistent service and community engagement.

As the Foundation grows, it aspires to expand its impact through additional welfare initiatives, educational support programs, healthcare assistance, rehabilitation services, and community development activities.

Our Vision

To build a society where children studying at the Mentally Retarded School, elderly people and those in need receive the care, support and respect they deserve. We hope to create a community where no child is neglected, no elderly person feels forgotten and everyone has the opportunity to live with dignity, happiness and hope.

Our Mission

To support children studying at the Mentally Retarded School, care for elderly people and help those in need through education, daily assistance, food support and community service. We are committed to providing care, respect and support to every individual we serve. Through the dedication of our team, volunteers and supporters, we strive to bring hope, comfort and a better quality of life to children, elderly people and families who need help.
